If you should have access to this, ensure you have associated your @pivotal.io email address to the GitHub account. You can find directions below:

How to associate an email address to GitHub

  1. Associate the email address used to sign the agreement to your GitHub account
  2. Verify your email address
  3. Sign into the new CLA tool. You must sign into the tool after you have linked your email address. This is necessary because, for privacy reasons, GitHub will not allow the tooling to view your email addresses without your consent.
  4. You may need to log out of the CLA tool and then log back in to update your email address.
